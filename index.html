Intro

Hi,

Hello, I'm Luthfi Muamar Azhar, founder of Soundperience, a venture dedicated to sound in audio-visual productions.

As a professional in the sound department for films and commercials, I offer a range of services including field recording, mixing engineering, and sound design. Continuously upgrading my skills through diverse experiences, I am committed to delivering authentic audio experiences in every project I handle. Let's transform sound into stories!

What I'm Doing

We bring stories to life through sound—capturing, editing, designing, scoring, creating, and mastering audio for an engaging experience.

Boom Operator & Location Sound Mixer

I capture clear and detailed audio during film and commercial shoots. My skills ensure natural and crisp dialogue and background sounds, enhancing the overall audio quality of the project. Using the right equipment and techniques, I deliver recordings ready for further production stages.

Dialogue Editing

I refine and enhance dialogue tracks to ensure they are clean and perfectly synced. Through precise editing, I remove unwanted noises and inconsistencies, delivering a seamless audio experience that elevates the overall production quality. .

Sound Design

I craft immersive soundscapes that bring stories to life. By blending various audio elements, I create a rich auditory environment that enhances the emotional impact and overall experience of any project.

Music Scoring

I compose original music that complements and elevates the narrative of your project. By creating custom scores that resonate with the story's emotions and themes, I ensure the music enhances the audience's connection and overall experience.

Foley & Voice Over Artist

I handle dialog editing, refining and syncing dialogue tracks for a clean and seamless audio experience. In addition, I create and capture foley sound effects to match on-screen actions, enhancing realism and immersiveness. I also provide professional voice over artists, ensuring the perfect voice to match your creative vision.

Mixing & Mastering

I meticulously balance and refine all audio elements, ensuring clarity, depth, and cohesion. Through detailed mixing and precise mastering, I deliver a polished final product that meets professional standards and elevates the overall sonic experience.
